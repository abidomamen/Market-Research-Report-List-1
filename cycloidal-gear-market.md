<p><ul><li>Single Stage Cycloidal Gearing</li><li>Double-Stage Cycloidal Gearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cycloidal gear market features two main types: single-stage and double-stage cycloidal gearing. Single-stage cycloidal gearing utilizes a single set of gears for torque transmission, offering simplicity and compact design, ideal for applications requiring moderate reduction ratios. In contrast, double-stage cycloidal gearing employs two sets of gears, providing higher reduction ratios and increased efficiency, making it suitable for more demanding applications. Both types are valued for their smooth operation, high torque capacity, and compact footprint, catering to diverse industries.</p></p>
