<p><ul><li>Tubular Sterilizer</li><li>Craped Surface Sterilizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra High Temperature (UHT) sterilizer market comprises primarily two types: Tubular sterilizers and Cramped surface sterilizers. Tubular sterilizers utilize long, narrow tubes for the rapid heating of liquids, promoting quick sterilization while minimizing product alteration. In contrast, Cramped surface sterilizers feature a compact design with large surface areas to enhance heat transfer efficiency, suitable for viscous products. Both types are crucial for extending shelf life and ensuring the safety of dairy products, juices, and other perishable goods.</p></p>
