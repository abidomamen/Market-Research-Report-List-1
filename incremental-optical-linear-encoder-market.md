<p><p>An Incremental Optical Linear Encoder is a precision measurement device that converts linear motion into a digital signal, using optical technology to detect position changes. It consists of a scale with alternating transparent and opaque patterns and a readhead that interprets these patterns to provide feedback on motion position, speed, and direction. These encoders are widely used in automation, robotics, CNC machines, and other industrial applications where precise positioning is essential.</p><p>The Incremental Optical Linear Encoder Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.7% during the forecast period.
