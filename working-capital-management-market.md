<p><strong>The Working Capital Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gross Working Capital (GWC)</li><li>Net Working Capital (NWC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Working Capital Management involves efficiently managing a company's short-term assets and liabilities to ensure liquidity and operational efficiency. The Gross Working Capital (GWC) market focuses on the total current assets, such as cash, inventory, and receivables, available to finance day-to-day operations. In contrast, the Net Working Capital (NWC) market subtracts current liabilities from current assets, providing a clearer picture of liquidity and financial health by reflecting the funds available for operational activities after accounting for obligations.</p></p>
