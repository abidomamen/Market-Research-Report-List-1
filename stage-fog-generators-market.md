<p><ul><li>Heated Fog Generators</li><li>Chilled Fog Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stage fog generators are essential in creating atmospheric effects for performances and events. Heated fog generators operate by vaporizing a special fluid, producing warm fog that rises and disperses, enhancing visibility and ambiance. In contrast, chilled fog generators utilize ice or chilled water to create denser fog that stays low to the ground, ideal for dramatic effects. Both types cater to different artistic needs, allowing event organizers to choose based on the desired visual impact and scene requirements.</p></p>
