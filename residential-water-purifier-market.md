<p><p>The residential water purifier market features various technologies to meet consumer needs. Multiple technology-based purifiers combine filtration methods like RO (reverse osmosis), UV (ultraviolet), and carbon filters for comprehensive purification. RO water purifiers are effective in removing dissolved impurities, ideal for areas with hard water. Gravity-based purifiers rely on natural filtration without electricity, making them cost-effective. UV water purifiers use ultraviolet light to eliminate bacteria and viruses, ensuring safe drinking water. Each type caters to diverse water quality challenges faced by households.</p></p>
