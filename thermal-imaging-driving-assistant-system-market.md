<p><p>The Thermal Imaging Driving Assistant System market comprises two main types: uncooled and cooled thermal imaging systems. Uncooled types utilize ambient temperature sensors, offering compact designs and lower costs, making them suitable for consumer vehicles. In contrast, cooled types employ cryogenic cooling to enhance sensitivity and thermal resolution, making them ideal for high-performance applications in commercial and military vehicles. Both types enhance driving safety by improving visibility in low-light conditions, contributing to advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) development.</p></p>
