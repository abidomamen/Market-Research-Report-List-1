<p><p>The Continuous Blood Glucose Monitoring market comprises two primary types: Self-Monitoring Blood Glucose (SMBG) systems and Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) systems. SMBG systems involve patients manually testing their blood glucose levels using portable devices, allowing for periodic monitoring and management. In contrast, CGM systems provide real-time glucose readings through wearable sensors that continuously track glucose levels, offering more comprehensive data and insights for diabetes management. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing patient care and optimizing treatment.</p></p>
