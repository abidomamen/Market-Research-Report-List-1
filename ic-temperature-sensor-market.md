<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The IC Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Output Temperature Sensors</li><li>Current Output Temperature Sensors</li><li>Digital Output Temperature Sensors</li><li>Resistance Output Silicon Temperature Sensors</li><li>Diode Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IC temperature sensor market encompasses various types tailored for specific applications. Voltage output temperature sensors provide a voltage that correlates with temperature changes, ideal for straightforward interfacing. Current output sensors deliver a proportional current for enhanced noise immunity. Digital output sensors utilize digital signals for precise temperature readings, often featuring built-in calibration. Resistance output silicon temperature sensors vary resistance with temperature, enabling accurate measurements. Lastly, diode temperature sensors leverage the temperature-dependent voltage drop across a diode for non-intrusive monitoring.</p></p>
