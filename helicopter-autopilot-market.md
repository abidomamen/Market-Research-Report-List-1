<p><p>The helicopter autopilot market comprises various types, each serving distinct functions. Attitude Hold maintains the desired aircraft orientation, enhancing stability. Coupled systems integrate with navigation for automated flight paths, improving precision and reducing pilot workload. Stability Augmentation Systems (SAS) assist in counteracting external disturbances, enhancing control in challenging conditions. Other systems may include advanced functionalities like hover hold and auto-landing, catering to diverse operational needs and contributing to increased safety and efficiency in helicopter operations.</p></p>
