<p><p>A servo motor is a rotary actuator that enables precise control of angular positioning, velocity, and acceleration. It consists of a motor coupled with a sensor for position feedback, which allows for high performance in applications that require accurate control. Servo motors are widely used in robotics, CNC machinery, conveyor systems, and aerospace applications.</p><p>The Servo Motor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.8% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing demand for automation across industries, as well as advancements in technology that enhance the efficiency and capabilities of servo systems. Industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and electronics are integrating servo motors to improve their production processes and product quality.</p><p>Recent trends indicate a rising focus on energy-efficient servo motors, with a shift towards brushless servo motors that offer higher reliability and lower maintenance costs. The integration of IoT technology is also becoming prevalent, facilitating smart manufacturing and enabling real-time monitoring of motor performance. Additionally, the growing adoption of servo motors in consumer electronics and robotics is contributing significantly to market expansion, highlighting their versatility and essential role in modern engineering solutions.</p></p>
