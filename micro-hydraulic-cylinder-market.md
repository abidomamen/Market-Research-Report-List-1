<p><strong>The Micro Hydraulic Cylinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting Hydraulic Cylinder</li><li>Double Acting Hydraulic Cylinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro hydraulic cylinder market features two main types: single acting and double acting hydraulic cylinders. Single acting cylinders utilize hydraulic pressure in one direction, typically employing a spring or gravity for return, making them simpler and cost-effective. In contrast, double acting cylinders facilitate movement in both directions, utilizing hydraulic pressure for extension and retraction, offering greater control and force. This versatility makes double acting cylinders widely used in applications requiring precise positioning and higher lifting capabilities.</p></p>
