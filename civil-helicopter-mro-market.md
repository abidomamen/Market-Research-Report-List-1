<p><p>The Civil Helicopter MRO market encompasses three primary types: Airframe Heavy Maintenance, Engine Maintenance, and Component Maintenance. Airframe Heavy Maintenance involves extensive inspections and repairs of the helicopter’s structure and systems, ensuring airworthiness. Engine Maintenance focuses on servicing and overhauling the helicopter engines to optimize performance and longevity. Component Maintenance deals with the repair and replacement of individual helicopter parts, such as avionics and hydraulic systems, essential for operational efficiency and safety. Each segment plays a vital role in maintaining helicopter reliability and compliance.</p></p>
