<p><p>In-Vehicle Payment Systems encompass various market types, including Embedded Systems, Mooring Systems, and Integrated Systems. Embedded Systems are integrated directly into vehicles, enabling seamless transactions via software. Mooring Systems involve fixed payment terminals at specific locations, allowing drivers to pay for services like parking or charging. Integrated Systems combine vehicle capabilities with external platforms, offering real-time payment solutions through mobile apps or smart devices. These systems aim to enhance convenience, efficiency, and user experience in automotive transactions.</p></p>
