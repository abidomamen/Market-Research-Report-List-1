<p><p>The Desktop Electronic Nose market comprises several technologies. Metal Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors detect gases through changes in resistance, offering high sensitivity and fast response times. Conducting Polymers (CP) utilize organic materials that change conductivity in response to volatile compounds, enabling versatile sensing applications. Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) measures mass changes on a crystal surface caused by gas adsorption, providing precise information on vapor concentration. Other market types may include hybrid sensors and advanced nanomaterials, enhancing detection capabilities and applications across various industries.</p></p>
