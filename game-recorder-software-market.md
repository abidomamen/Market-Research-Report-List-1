<p><p>Game recorder software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based game recorders allow users to capture gameplay and store or access recordings remotely via the internet, providing flexibility and ease of use. On-premise software, in contrast, is installed directly on a user’s device, ensuring that recordings are stored locally and often offering more control over processing power and features. Both options cater to varying user needs for accessibility and system requirements.</p></p>
