<p><p>The combustion control equipment market comprises three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic systems. Manual systems require operators to adjust and monitor combustion parameters, providing flexibility and control but relying heavily on human intervention. Semi-automatic systems enhance efficiency by allowing partial automation, enabling some parameters to be set automatically while still requiring human oversight. Fully automatic systems offer complete automation, optimizing combustion processes with minimal human interaction, ensuring consistent performance and efficiency, and minimizing emissions and fuel consumption.</p></p>
