<p><strong>The Levitation Induction Melting Furnac Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coreless Induction Furnace</li><li>Cold Crucible Induction Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The levitation induction melting furnace market includes two main types: coreless induction furnaces and cold crucible induction furnaces. Coreless induction furnaces use electromagnetic fields to melt materials without a fixed core, providing flexibility and efficient energy use. In contrast, cold crucible induction furnaces utilize a non-contact melting approach, where the molten metal is contained in a conductive crucible that remains cool, enabling precise temperature control and reduced contamination. Both types are pivotal in industries requiring high-purity metal processing.</p></p>
