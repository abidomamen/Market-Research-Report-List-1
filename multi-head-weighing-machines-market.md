<p><p>The multi-head weighing machines market is primarily divided into two types: linear multi-head weighing machines and rotary multi-head weighing machines. Linear multi-head machines operate in a straight line, providing quick and accurate measurements, making them suitable for high-speed processing of small to medium-sized products. In contrast, rotary multi-head weighing machines use a circular design, enhancing versatility and efficiency, ideal for bulkier items. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring precision in packaging and production processes.</p></p>
