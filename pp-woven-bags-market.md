<p><p>The PP woven bags market is primarily divided into two types: laminated and non-laminated bags. Laminated PP woven bags feature a protective film that enhances durability, moisture resistance, and printability, making them ideal for high-quality packaging and storage needs. Non-laminated PP woven bags, on the other hand, lack this coating and are typically more cost-effective, allowing for breathability and versatility in various applications, including agriculture and bulk transport. Each type serves distinct market demands based on functionality and budget.</p></p>
