"5-axis" machines allow for more complex shapes and are capable of machining multiple sides of a workpiece in one setup, enhancing precision and efficiency. "More than 5-axis" centers provide advanced multi-axis capabilities, enabling intricate designs and contours, making them ideal for high-precision industries like aerospace and medical manufacturing.</p></p>
