<p><p>The Platform Automatic Guided Vehicle (AGV) market consists of various navigation types. Laser navigation uses laser beams for precise positioning and mapping, offering flexibility and adaptability in dynamic environments. Magnetic navigation relies on magnetic strips placed on the floor to guide AGVs, providing a reliable and cost-effective solution. Visual navigation employs cameras and sensors to interpret surroundings, enabling advanced obstacle detection. Other navigation methods may include inertial navigation and ultrasonic guidance, catering to diverse operational needs and enhancing automation efficiency across industries.</p></p>
