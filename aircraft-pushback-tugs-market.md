<p><strong>The Aircraft Pushback Tugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional/Towbars Pushback Tugs</li><li>Towbarless Pushback Tugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft pushback tugs market includes two main types: conventional/towbar pushback tugs and towbarless pushback tugs. Conventional pushback tugs utilize a towbar to connect to the aircraft's nose landing gear, providing a secure method for maneuvering heavier aircraft. In contrast, towbarless pushback tugs attach directly to the aircraft, offering greater agility and quicker turnaround times. Both types are essential for efficient aircraft operations at airports, catering to different operational needs and aircraft designs.</p></p>
