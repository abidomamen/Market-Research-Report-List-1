<p><strong>The Automotive and Transportation Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire to Board Connector</li><li>Board to Board Connector</li><li>Wire to Wire Connector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive and Transportation Connector Market encompasses various types, including wire-to-board, board-to-board, and wire-to-wire connectors. Wire-to-board connectors facilitate connections between wire harnesses and circuit boards, essential for integrating electrical systems in vehicles. Board-to-board connectors link two circuit boards, optimizing space and improving signal integrity in complex automotive applications. Wire-to-wire connectors interconnect multiple wire harnesses, ensuring reliable power and signal transmission. Each connector type plays a critical role in enhancing the performance, safety, and reliability of automotive and transportation systems.</p></p>
