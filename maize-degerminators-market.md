<p><strong>The Maize Degerminators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Type</li><li>Vertical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Maize degerminators are categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on their design and operational method. Horizontal degerminators typically feature a cylindrical shape with two horizontal rollers that grind maize kernels, effectively removing germ and bran. In contrast, vertical degerminators utilize a vertical configuration with an auger-driven design to achieve a similar effect, allowing for better control over the process. These distinct designs cater to varying production needs and efficiency levels within the maize processing industry.</p></p>
