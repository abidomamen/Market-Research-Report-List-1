<p><p>Large Format Displays (LFD) are oversized screens used for digital signage and presentations, encompassing various technologies. LED-Backlit LCDs use LED lights for backlighting, offering vibrant colors and energy efficiency. Direct-View LEDs consist of individual LED modules, providing exceptional brightness and flexibility for large installations. OLED displays deliver superior contrast and color richness due to their self-emissive nature. E-Paper technology mimics printed paper, providing low power consumption and high readability, ideal for applications requiring static information and extended battery life.</p></p>
