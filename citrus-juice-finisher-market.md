<p><strong>The Citrus Juice Finisher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw-Based</li><li>Paddle Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Citrus Juice Finisher market comprises primarily two types: screw-based and paddle-based systems. Screw-based finishers utilize a helical screw mechanism to extract juice, ensuring efficient separation of pulp and juice while maintaining product quality. In contrast, paddle-based finishers employ rotating paddles to mix and agitate the citrus material, enhancing juice extraction and clarity. Both types cater to varying production capacities and operational efficiencies, allowing manufacturers to choose based on specific processing requirements and desired juice characteristics.</p></p>
