Ultraviolet Mercury Lamp Disinfection utilizes traditional mercury vapor lamps, delivering high-intensity UVC light effective in killing pathogens over larger areas. In contrast, UVC-LED Disinfection employs light-emitting diodes for a more energy-efficient, compact solution with quicker switching capabilities, making it suitable for various applications. Both technologies aim to enhance hygiene and safety in environments like hospitals, offices, and public spaces.</p></p>
