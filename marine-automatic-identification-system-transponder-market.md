<p><p>The Marine Automatic Identification System (AIS) Transponder market is primarily divided into Class A and Class B AIS types. Class A AIS is mandated for commercial vessels over 300 gross tons and provides real-time tracking, navigation, and safety data, enhancing maritime awareness. In contrast, Class B AIS is designed for smaller vessels and recreational boats, offering essential positional information but with less frequent updates and lower power requirements compared to Class A, making it cost-effective and suitable for non-commercial applications.</p></p>
