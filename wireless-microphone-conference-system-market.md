<p><p>The Wireless Microphone Conference System market consists of several types of microphones tailored for effective audio capture in meetings and conferences. Ceiling microphones are installed on ceilings for unobtrusive audio pickup. Gooseneck microphones offer flexibility in positioning and are often used in podiums. Boundary microphones are placed on surfaces, capturing sound from around the area. Speaker phones facilitate hands-free communication, allowing participants to engage without holding a device. Each type enhances audio clarity and user experience in conferencing environments.</p></p>
