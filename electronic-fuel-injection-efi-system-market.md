<p><strong>The Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Point Injection</li><li>Continuous Injection</li><li>Central Port Injection</li><li>Multipoint Fuel Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li><li>Swirl Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) system market encompasses several types of fuel delivery methods. Single-Point Injection uses a single injector per engine bank, while Continuous Injection maintains a steady flow of fuel. Central Port Injection places injectors at the intake manifold, whereas Multipoint Fuel Injection employs multiple injectors for more precise fuel delivery. Direct Injection sends fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing efficiency. Swirl Injection creates turbulence for better atomization and combustion, optimizing performance and emissions across various engine types.</p></p>
