<p><ul><li>Ordinary Bias Tires</li><li>Bias Belted Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle bias tire market consists of two primary types: ordinary bias tires and bias belted tires. Ordinary bias tires have diagonal plies that provide flexibility and a comfortable ride, making them suitable for light-duty applications. In contrast, bias belted tires incorporate additional belts beneath the tread, enhancing stability, traction, and durability while offering better handling at higher speeds. Both tire types cater to different performance needs, with bias belted tires often preferred for heavier vehicles and demanding driving conditions.</p></p>
