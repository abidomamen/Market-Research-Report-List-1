<p><p>The continuous reactors market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Tubular reactors facilitate high-throughput reactions in a streamlined manner, ideal for gas-phase processes. Fixed bed reactors utilize stationary packing for solid catalysts, offering efficient mass transfer for heterogeneous reactions. Fluid bed reactors excel in handling fine particles and suspensions through fluidization, enhancing heat and mass transfer. Continuous stirred tank reactors (CSTR) maintain uniform compositions via continuous mixing, making them suitable for liquid-phase reactions needing consistent product quality.</p></p>
