<p><strong>The Smart Dimming Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic</li><li>Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal</li><li>Suspended Particles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Dimming Glass market features three main types: Electrochromic, Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC), and Suspended Particle Devices (SPD). Electrochromic glass changes opacity when an electrical charge is applied, providing energy efficiency and privacy. PDLC technology uses liquid crystals that align or disperse in response to voltage, allowing for instant changes in transparency. SPD technology employs suspended particles that shift between clear and opaque states when an electric current is introduced, enhancing light control and aesthetics.</p></p>
